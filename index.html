My current interests

The latest technology to peak my interests is Apple, who is moving their MAC computers to their own custom made processors that are based on the ARM Architecture. I have mixed emotions about such as I appreciate the agility that currently comes with intel processors in MAC which have enabled Windows 10 to run on MAC (Bootcamp), but nonetheless I see the whole industry transforming to embrace this technology due to the amount of performance per watt these sort of processors can bring to our devices. Moreover, Microsoft seems to be interested in ARM and are also working closely with Qualcomm to also embrace ARM based processors.
